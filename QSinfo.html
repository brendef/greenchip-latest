Quantity Surveying Services

Construction cost consultants with expert knowledge of construction economics throughout the lifecycle of the project: 

Project Inception 

We collaborate and co-operation with design team members (architects, consulting engineers and other professional consultants) to establish cost estimates, budgets and other cost advice information. 

Concept & Viability

We proactively determine the size, standard and cost of projects using economies of project scale and preparation of budgets.  

Design Development

We prepare elemental or other standard forms of cost planning linked to design development and budget allocation to various construction components or project phases. 

Documentation & Procurement

We prepare and evaluate tender documents, including the advantages of bills of quantities and contract administration documents. 

Construction

We implement financial control during the construction process by undertaking monthly payment certificates and calculating price fluctuations in terms of the applicable contract price adjustment provision to ensure that budgets are well managed. 

Project Close-out

We compile final accounts for the main contract and negotiate with contractors and subcontractors until agreements are reached.
